What is DazzlingBAD?

DazzlingBAD is a Japanese v系 (visual kei) band, that started initially as a cover session band, HATEman, in 2018, and debuted as a new unit on October 2020. Their name signifies beauty in values that are generally considered negative. They’d rather love people who live in a world full of “BAD” than with beauty. Musically, they’re part of the loud kei subgenre.
